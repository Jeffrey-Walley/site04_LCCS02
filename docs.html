The History of LCCS

My Love for Dogs and the Lord were the prime motivations for starting the Louisiana Christian Canine Society.

I introduced my own four legged friend to the word of the Lord through Praise worship and could instantly see his soul brighten.

That's when Champ and I decided to Prostilatize His word and Spread the Joy of the Lord.

Champ, my Weimaraner, passed of Covid-19 in 2020 and I decided to Create the LCCS as a way to remember my best friend, and one of the Lord's greatest missionaries. Who knew Weimaraners could be such great shepherds!
